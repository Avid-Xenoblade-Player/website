What Is Simon Doing?

What is this Game?

In the school puzzle game “What is Simon Doing?”, players embark on a quest to hack into a mysterious laptop. They collect items like keys and money by solving puzzles and completing challenges scattered throughout the school. Each item helps players get one step closer to solving the puzzle, ultimately revealing the laptop’s secrets and uncovering Simon’s true intentions.

Features

  • Different Locations
  • Items to collect
  • Talk To NPCs
  • Puzzles
  • Learn about Cyber Security
  • Puzzle Solving
