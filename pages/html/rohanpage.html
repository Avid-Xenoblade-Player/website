Turnabout Phishing

Step into the shoes of Phoenix Wright in this thrilling Ace Attorney case where two old best friends, Billy Boo and Willy Woo, are caught up in a catfishing scandal.

Willy Woo claims that Billy Boo framed him on the popular social media site Racebook, but Phoenix Wright has doubts. It's up to you to help Phoenix identify the contradictions in Willy Woo's testimony. Was Billy really the catfisher, or is Willy hiding something?
