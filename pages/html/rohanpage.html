What's Turnabout Phishing About?

Step into the role of Phoenix Wright in this thrilling courtroom drama! In *Turnabout Phishing*, Willy Woo accuses his former best friend, Billy Boo, of framing him for a catfishing scandal. Your task? To uncover the truth behind the case and expose the real culprit. Full of unexpected twists and dramatic cross-examinations, it's up to you to piece together the contradictions and reveal who is really pulling the strings.
